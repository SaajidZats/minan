Inspiring Young Hearts with the Light of Deen Since the Early 1980s

Reg. No. CI 016 0124 - MRCA/AHA/CI/016
Approved by the Department of Muslim Religious and Cultural Affairs

Our Mission

To provide quality Islamic education to our students, nurturing them with the teachings of the Qur'an and Sunnah while fostering discipline, compassion, and community responsibility.

Our Vision

To build a generation rooted in Islamic values, empowered with knowledge and integrity to serve the Ummah and contribute positively to society.

Our Leadership

Since 1985, Moulavi Ash-Sheikh M. Irshard Zawahir (Bari) has led as principal, guiding with humility and teamwork. The school's success owes much to generous contributions—teacher allowances, uniforms, and volunteer support—from the community.
