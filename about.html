About Us

Minan Ahadhiyya School has been inspiring young hearts with the light of Deen since the early 1980s. Founded through heartfelt discussions among respected community leaders—late Mawsook Haji, Haji Jiffry Buhard, and Muhyiddeen Mosque’s administration—the school was created to uplift the Islamic knowledge of Muslim children in Dematagoda.

From a small group of 20–30 students led by Ustad Siddique Alim, the school has grown into a respected institution. Officially registered in 2008 as the 103rd Ahadhiyya in Sri Lanka, Minan has long been a pillar of faith and education.
