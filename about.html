Our History

Madrasathul Minan Ahadhiyya was established over three decades ago with the goal of strengthening Islamic education in the Dematagoda region. Initiated by respected elders and mosque leaders, it evolved into a dedicated Sunday religious institution offering structured learning from Grade 6 to 11.

Current Situation

Our Ahadhiyya functions actively with a weekly schedule, dedicated teachers, and a progressive syllabus. Every Sunday, over a hundred students benefit from quality Islamic education, character development, and spiritual guidance.
